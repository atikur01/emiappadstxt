1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how EMI Interest Calculator (the "App") collects, uses, and protects information. The App is provided by the developer and is intended to be used on mobile devices.

2. Information Collected

The App is designed to operate entirely offline and does not collect, transmit, or store personal information about its users. Specifically:

  • No personal data: We do not request or store names, email addresses, phone numbers, or other identifiers.
  • No telemetry: The App does not include analytics, crash reporting, or usage-tracking libraries.
  • Local calculations only: All EMI calculations and related data remain on the user's device and are not uploaded to any server.

3. How We Use Information

Because the App does not collect personal information, there is no user information used, processed, or shared by the developer. All inputs entered into the App are used only locally to compute results for the user's convenience.

4. Third‑Party Services

The App does not integrate with third‑party advertising or analytics services. There are no SDKs embedded that would transmit data to external parties.

5. Security

Because no data leaves the user's device, the primary security consideration is the device itself. Users are encouraged to follow standard device security practices (screen lock, backups, OS updates) to keep local data safe.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for use by all ages and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information in error, please contact us (see section 8).

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect app changes or legal requirements. When we make material changes we will update the "Effective date" at the top of this document. Continued use of the App after such changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Policy.
